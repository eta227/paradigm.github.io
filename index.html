Paradigm

The human being as a product and a producer of culture — as expressed in Nietzsche works — is the idea that we want to explore in the context of the new digital era we are living in

Objectives

Our purpose is to create an immersive experience where the user, young adults, can explore the antithesis of a world saturated with digital stimuli and a world of absence of those same stimuli.

Reflections

These extreme emotional and psychological situations are the perfect scenarios to promote a reflection in which users can place themselves in relation to the new digital culture we are living in. If we create a standard where we define in one extreme being completely addicted to digital technology and on the other side refusing interaction with technology, where will you be?
